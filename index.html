Benefits of Caret Juice Funnel Optimization

Quicker time to value

We use lightweight, structured interviews to gain insights focused on specific conversions. This lets us start improving your funnel sooner with less up-front discovery time.

Less waste

We aim to produce shippable funnel improvements every two weeks. To meet this goal we have to be efficient when testing insights and limit unnecessary work.

Bigger improvements

Your analytics, marketing automation, and CRM data tells you who and what. Structured interviews tell you why. The insights into why people interact help us lift conversion rates and accelerate your funnels.

Faster test results

By running tests that challenge why people interact, we aim for large conversion rate gains which quickly get you significant results even at B-to-B sample sizes.

Optimize tricky pages

A/B testing for home pages and product pages is a challenge because people visit those pages for many reasons that don’t result in easily measurable conversions. Asking why people visit gives you the information you need to create better interactions.
